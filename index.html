Overview

Fiber Splice Recorder is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Fiber Splice Recorder does not collect, transmit, or share any user data.

All your data including:

  • Fiber closures and locations
  • Cable configurations (TIA-598-D and IEC 60304 standards)
  • Splice connections and matrices
  • Budget calculations and link losses
  • Exported PDF reports
  • All preferences and settings

...are stored locally on your device only.

What We Don't Do

We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track user behavior or analytics
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use advertising or marketing trackers
  • Transmit your data to any servers
  • Access your data in any way

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using:

  • Encrypted local storage (AES-256-GCM)
  • iOS Keychain for secure key management
  • Local device storage only
  • No cloud synchronization
  • No external servers

Your data remains private and under your complete control.

Your data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption with keys stored securely in iOS Keychain. This is the same encryption standard used by banks and government agencies.

Data Security

Since all data stays on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception
  • No external access to your information
  • Industry-standard AES-256-GCM encryption

Industry Standards Compliance

Fiber Splice Recorder adheres to the following industry standards:

  • TIA-598-D: Standard for color coding of fiber optic cables
  • IEC 60304: Standard color sequence for telecommunications
  • ANSI/TIA-568.3-E: Fiber optic standards and testing

These standards are implemented locally for color coding and cable management only. No data related to these standards is transmitted or shared.

Third-Party Services

Fiber Splice Recorder does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Fiber Splice Recorder does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect any information from anyone.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data:

  • There is no data to access, modify, or delete
  • All your data is already under your control on your device
  • You can delete all app data by deleting the app from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
